Merging Indonesia's PODES Waves

Crosswalks and do-files for merging multiple waves of the Indonesian village census (PODES) spanning 1993, 1996, 2000, 2003, 2005, 2008, 2011, and 2014. The materials also facilitate merging PODES data with village population counts from the 2010 population census. Additional crosswalks cover 2013–2021 for users integrating PODES 2014 with PODES 2018–2021.

PODES 1993–2014 Merge (Dropbox)

A detailed merge procedure is documented in the Data Appendix of:

Cassidy, Traviss (2025): "Revenue Persistence and Public Service Delivery," Economic Journal, 135(670): 2017–2053.

If you use these materials, please cite the paper above.
